Overview

Purpose

This website provides the perfect place to design, search for and share workouts. It has a range of targeted workouts for any area of your body and will help anyone on their fitness journey.

Audience

This is meant for anyone in any stage of a fitness journey, from someone who rarely leaves the house or doesn't ever work out, to someone who thinks of Mount Everest as the warm-up to the rest of their workout.

Content

Home page

People will visit the site for all kinds of reasons, all based around personal fitness. Someone might come to the site because they would like to learn how to create a balanced workout that will feel good. Others might come to the website to gain ideas for what new techniques they can try in the gym. Even others may come to the site to motivate themselves to just go to the gym. There will be many good health reasons to visit the website.

Featured workouts (trending, new, editor's picks) "Browse by Category" section with visual icons (strength, cardio, yoga, etc.) Brief "About Us" or mission statement blurb

Robust Search & Filtering

Search by keyword, workout type, muscle group, equipment, time commitment, difficulty Filter results with multiple selections (e.g., "bodyweight" + "cardio" +"20 minutes") Workout Creation (Optional)

Guided form for users to input their workout routines. Image/video upload Ability to tag workouts with relevant keywords for better searchability. Social Interaction

Comment sections under workouts Sharing workouts to external social media platforms Possibly a "follow" feature for users who create popular workouts User Experience Considerations

Clean & Uncluttered Design: The focus should be on easily finding workouts. Avoid overwhelming users with too much information on one screen. Visuals Matter: High-quality workout images and videos are essential for engagement. Personalization (if possible): Track workout preferences and suggest workouts fitting a user's history or saved workouts.
